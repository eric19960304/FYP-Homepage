About Us

Our Vision

Ride-sharing could help Hong Kong realise its full potential as a smart city. However, there is no ride-sharing app or platform that is reasonably popularised in our city so far. Our mission is to develop an easy-to-use ride-sharing app for HK using cutting-edge algorithmic from recent academic research. We want to provide a convenient, reliable, and economic mean to hongkongers for daily commutating to workplace or to school, and thus improve the Hong Kong Public Transportation System.
